Welcome to Colombia

Explore vibrant cities, lush landscapes, and rich culture in the heart of South America.

Must-Visit Places

Cartagena

A colorful colonial city by the Caribbean. Walk the old town walls, enjoy the beaches, and explore vibrant nightlife.

Medellín

The City of Eternal Spring. Take the cable cars over the mountains, enjoy art installations, and discover its innovative urban culture.

Bogotá

Colombia’s bustling capital. Visit Monserrate for panoramic views, explore La Candelaria, and experience the rich cultural heritage.

Iconic Foods to Try

Arepas

Cornmeal cakes that are grilled or fried, often filled with cheese, meats, or eggs. A staple for any Colombian meal.

Bandeja Paisa

A hearty platter with beans, rice, avocado, plantain, egg, meat, and chorizo. A traditional dish from the Antioquia region.

Colombian Coffee

World-renowned coffee beans grown in lush mountains. Enjoy a fresh cup in traditional cafés or during farm tours.

Essential Travel Tips

Use Local Transport Apps

Uber and Beat are widely used in cities like Bogotá and Medellín, making travel easier and safer.

Stay Hydrated and Protect from Sun

Especially in coastal areas like Cartagena and Santa Marta. Bring sunscreen and drink bottled water when in doubt.

Learn Basic Spanish Phrases

English is not always widely spoken outside tourist areas, so knowing greetings and polite phrases is helpful.

Be Mindful of Safety

Avoid poorly lit streets at night and stay aware in crowded tourist spots. Stick to recommended areas by locals or guides.
