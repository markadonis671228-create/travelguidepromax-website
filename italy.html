Welcome to Italy

Experience art, history, and cuisine in the heart of the Mediterranean.

Must-Visit Places

Rome

The Eternal City. Visit the Colosseum, Vatican City, and walk through centuries of history.

Venice

The city on water. Explore the canals, St. Mark’s Square, and the glassworks of Murano.

Florence

Home of the Renaissance. See the Uffizi Gallery, Michelangelo’s David, and the beautiful Duomo.

Iconic Foods to Try

Pizza Margherita

Simple and perfect — mozzarella, tomato, and basil baked on a wood-fired crust.

Pasta

From carbonara to tagliatelle al ragù, each region has its own signature pasta dish.

Gelato

Creamier than ice cream, Italian gelato is a must-try treat on a warm day.

Essential Travel Tips

Validate Your Train Ticket

Validate your ticket before boarding regional trains to avoid fines.

Dress Modestly in Churches

Cover shoulders and knees when visiting religious sites in Italy.

Eat Like a Local

Lunch is from 1–3 PM, dinner starts late. Enjoy meals slowly and socially.

Watch for Pickpockets

Be alert and keep your belongings close in crowded tourist areas.
