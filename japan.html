Welcome to Japan

Discover the perfect blend of ancient tradition and modern innovation.

Must-Visit Places

Tokyo

The bustling capital. Explore the neon-lit streets of Shinjuku, the serene Meiji Shrine, and the trendy shops of Harajuku.

Kyoto

Japan's cultural heart. Visit stunning temples like Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Fushimi Inari Shrine, and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

Osaka

Known as Japan's kitchen. Dive into the vibrant food scene in Dotonbori, visit the historic Osaka Castle, and enjoy Universal Studios Japan.

Iconic Foods to Try

Sushi & Sashimi

Experience the freshness of raw fish, whether it's expertly placed on vinegared rice (sushi) or served on its own (sashimi).

Ramen

A hearty bowl of noodle soup. From rich tonkotsu (pork bone) to light shoyu (soy sauce) broth, every region has its specialty.

Tempura

Lightly battered and deep-fried seafood and vegetables. Perfectly crispy, it's often served with a dipping sauce or over rice.

Essential Travel Tips

Get a Japan Rail (JR) Pass

If you plan on traveling between cities, the JR Pass saves money on Shinkansen (bullet train) fares.

Stay Connected

Rent a pocket Wi-Fi or get a tourist SIM for easy navigation and communication.

Carry Cash

While cards work in big cities, many small restaurants and shops accept only cash.

Learn Basic Etiquette

Bowing is common, stay quiet on trains, and remember that tipping is not practiced in Japan.
